Meet Maya Whitely

Hi, I’m Maya — a confidence coach for women who are ambitious, thoughtful, and often stuck in their own heads.

I work with women who look capable on the outside but feel unsure on the inside. Women who overthink every decision, second-guess their voice, and quietly wonder if they’re “too much” or “not enough” at the same time.

My work is about helping you slow the mental noise, reconnect with your inner confidence, and move forward with clarity — without forcing yourself to become someone you’re not.

You don’t need to be louder. You need to feel safer being yourself.

This Coaching Is for You If…

  • You’re ambitious but constantly second-guess yourself
  • You overthink conversations, decisions, and next steps
  • You’re sensitive, intuitive, and emotionally aware
  • You want confidence that feels calm — not aggressive
  • You’re ready to trust yourself and speak from alignment

Confidence doesn’t have to feel heavy. It can feel steady.
